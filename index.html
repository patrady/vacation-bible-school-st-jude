About True North VBS

At True North, kids discover that they can trust Jesus in a wild world! True North is filled with awesome Bible-learning experiences kids see, hear, and touch! Hands-on science experiments, team-building games, unforgettable Bible songs, and tasty treats are just a few of the standout activities that help faith flow into real life. (Since everything is hands-on, kids might get a little messy. Be sure to send them in play clothes and sturdy shoes.) Volunteer With Us

Teen and adult volunteers are needed to help make this year's VBS a success! Adult volunteers MUST be Safe-Environment certified. If you're unsure if your training is up to date, please contact the Parish Office to find out.

Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Elementary Crew Leader: Lead a small group of kids from station to station.
  • Awesome Bible Adventures: Explore each day's Bible story and help bring the story to life.
  • Tundra Treats: Help prepare and present treats to all the kids that help illustrate what they're learning.
  • Glacier Games: Take part in outdoor games and water activities to get the kids moving! You can expect lots of hard work, fun, and high-fives.
  • Sticky Scripture Cinema: Introduce the daily Bible Memory Buddy.
  • Mountain Music: Lead singing and dancing to age-specific songs.
  • Compass Crafts: Encourage creativity and bring the Bible to life through hands-on crafts.
  • Registration: Assist with registration, check in, and collecting registration fees.
  • Photography: Take photos to document the key events from each day.

Volunteer Registration Deadline: May 22, 2026
