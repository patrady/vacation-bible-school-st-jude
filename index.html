About True North VBS

Welcome to True North VBS - where adventure meets faith! This year's outdoor-themed Vacation Bible School will take children on an unforgettable journey through God's creation.

Surrounded by the beauty of nature - from towering pine trees to majestic mountains, from playful otters to soaring eagles - children will discover that Jesus is our True North, guiding us through every adventure life brings.

Through engaging Bible stories, hands-on crafts, exciting games, and memorable music, kids will learn how to navigate life with Jesus as their compass, always pointing them in the right direction.
